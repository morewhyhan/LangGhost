# LangGhost

LangGhost is obsidian plugin for people who want write english good.

You type sentence. It check automatic when you finish sentence with `.` or `?` or `!`. Error show with wave line. Press `Ctrl+Alt+.` to fix nearset error, `Ctrl+Shift+.` fix whole sentence. Or hover wave line and click apply.

No need perfict english to start. just start write. the plugin help you along the way. evrey mistake you fix is one step close to fluently.
